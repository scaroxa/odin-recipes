Recipe to make flying jacob

In this recipe we will be making Flying Jacob, a swedish dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken fillets (about 150g / 5 oz each)
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 140g (5 oz) diced bacon
  • 1 banana
  • 250ml (1 cup) cooking cream
  • 100ml (1/3-1/2 cup) chili sauce (ketchup-style chili sauce, not hot sauce)
  • Rice or couscous for 4 servings
  • 100ml (1/3-1/2 cup) salted peanuts

    • For serving

  • Salad

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 225°C (435°F)
  2. Cut the chicken into strips. Brown the chicken in the oil in a frying pan in batches. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to an ovenproof dish.
  3. Fry the bacon and place it on top of the chicken.
  4. Peel and slice the banana and lay the slices over the chicken.
  5. Mix the cream and chili sauce. Taste and adjust with salt and pepper. Pour the mixture over the chicken.
  6. Bake in the middle of the oven for about 20 minutes.
  7. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  8. Sprinkle the peanuts over the chicken before serving. Serve with rice and a salad.
